### 📖 Biography
I am a researcher and developer dedicated to exploring the boundaries of artificial intelligence. My research primarily focuses on Large Language Models (LLMs), synthetic data evaluation, and unsupervised anomaly detection algorithms. While deeply engaged in academic research, I also build practical utility tools to streamline workflows and improve productivity.
